# Project Overview
FreelanceGig Analytics is a Tableau dashboard project that analyzes trends in the gig economy. It explores freelancer earnings, job success rates, client ratings, rehire rates, and platform performance across different freelance platforms. 

The dashboard uses interactive charts, visualizations, KPIs, and filters to understand freelancer performance, compare platforms, and identify high-paying job categories. It also provides insights into experience levels, rehire rates, and performance to support better business and career decisions.

# Problem Statement
Gig economy is growing rapidly, but freelancers and clients often struggle to understand job success patterns, identify high-paying freelance categories, the demand for certain skils and analyze freelancer performance trends.

# Project Objectives
Analyze freelancer job trends
Assess freelancer earnings
Compare platform performance
Identify freelancer success factors

# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* **Total Freelancer:** 1,950 Freelancers.
* **Total Jobs:** 294,185 Jobs completed
* **Total Platform Revenue:** $9,784,255 (~$9.78M).
* **Quality Benchmarks:** 4.0/5.0 Average Client Rating with a 75% Average Job Success Rate.
* **Market Rate:** $52.60 average hourly rate.
  

# Key Insights
- Intermediate freelancers earn higher hourly at fixed rates than Expert freelancers.
- Fixed rate projects often generate higher earnings than hourly rate projects.
- Graphics Design, App Development, Customer Support and Web Development categories produce the highest earnings.
- Platform performance differs across job categories.
- Toptal, Freelancer and Fiverr have strong rehire rates, though neither platform leads in number of freelancers.
- Upwork and PeoplePerHour have the highest and lowest freelancer counts respectively, but both show the lowest rehire rates.
- Higher job success rates highlight the importance of platform quality rather than simply the number of freelancers available. Platforms with the highest success rates, such as Freelancer, Toptal, and Fiverr, do not have the largest pools of freelancers.
- Higher client ratings are associated with better rehire rates.
- Web Development and Digital Marketing holds the highest average client ratings, demonstrating strong satisfaction and excellent service in this category.
- SEO has the lowest client ratings and earnings, suggesting there are opportunities to enhance service quality in this category.

# Business Recommendations
- Categories like Web Development, Graphics Design, and App Development show stronger earnings. Freelancers should build skills in these areas to increase income potential.
- Encourage freelancers to improve client ratings and job success rates by focusing on quality work, fast delivery, good communication and meeting deadlines.
- Beginners should target platforms with higher success rates such as Freelancer, Toptal and Fiverr.
- Freelancers can increase profits by targeting fixed rate contracts.
- Different platforms perform better for different job categories. Freelancers should choose platforms that match their skills and target audience.
- Increase marketing efforts in high-performing categories.
